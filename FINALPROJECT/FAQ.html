FAQs

What are the admission requirements?
The admission requirements include completing the application form, submitting relevant documents, and attending an interview (if applicable).
How do I apply for financial aid?
To apply for financial aid, complete the financial assistance form available on our website and submit it along with your application.
What extracurricular activities are available?
Our school offers a variety of extracurricular activities, including sports, music, arts, and academic clubs to enhance students' overall development.
When is the application deadline?
The application deadline is typically in mid-March for the upcoming academic year.
