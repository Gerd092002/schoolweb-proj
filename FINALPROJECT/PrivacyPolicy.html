Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

Information We Collect

We collect information from students, parents, and staff to ensure smooth operations and effective communication. This includes names, contact information, academic records, and any other details required for enrollment, administration, and educational purposes.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to manage academic records, provide school services, and communicate important updates. We do not share your data with third parties without consent, except as required by law.

How We Protect Your Information

We implement appropriate security measures to safeguard your data. These measures include secure databases, restricted access to sensitive information, and regular audits to prevent unauthorized use or disclosure.

Use of Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies help us analyze web traffic and tailor content to your preferences. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies.

Parental Consent

For students under the age of 18, we require parental or guardian consent before collecting or using personal data. This ensures compliance with applicable child protection laws.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be communicated through our website and other appropriate channels. We encourage you to review this page regularly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us through our Contact Us page.
