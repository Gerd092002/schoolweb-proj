School Academic Approach

Our School focuses on providing a balanced education through comprehensive Junior High School and Senior High School programs. We aim to develop both academic excellence and character growth.

Programs We Offer

Junior High School

Designed for Grades 7 to 10, our Junior High School program builds foundational skills across core subjects while encouraging holistic development.

Senior High School

For Grades 11 and 12, Senior High School offers academic tracks tailored to prepare students for college and careers, including STEM, ABM, and ICT tracks.
