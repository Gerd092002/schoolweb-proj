Code of Conduct

We expect all students, staff, and visitors to maintain a respectful and inclusive environment. Any form of bullying, discrimination, or misconduct will not be tolerated and will result in disciplinary action.

Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. Any personal information collected through forms or interactions will be used strictly for educational purposes and will not be shared without consent. For details, refer to our Privacy Policy page.

Anti-Bullying Policy

Our school has a zero-tolerance approach to bullying. We provide support for affected students and ensure that all reported incidents are addressed promptly and effectively.

Attendance Policy

Regular attendance is crucial for academic success. Students are expected to attend all classes and activities unless excused by a valid reason. Excessive absenteeism may affect academic standing.

Grievance Policy

We are committed to addressing grievances in a fair and timely manner. Students, parents, and staff may report issues confidentially through our Contact Us page.
